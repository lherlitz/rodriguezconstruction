HSE

Health, Safety & Enviromental

Our certified team ensures that the job gets done safely, reliably, and at a cost-effective price.
Family owned and operated, Rodriguez Construction knows that the best outcomes for our customers will be achieved through our employees’ collaborative spirit and sense of pride in their work.

Safety and quality procedures exceed industry standards. Our team is certified to ensure that safety is a priority. As we partner with you during the project, we provide a competitive advantage on every project because we know our industry – inside and out.

Daily Safety Meetings: Before work begins, we perform a daily safety meeting. Here is where we conduct a job safety analysis (JSA) and make sure everyone is aware of the hazards. Our safety meetings are target specific, and industry oriented so that our staff is acutely aware of all current issues facing the industry.
 
Weekly Safety Meetings: In addition to our daily job site meetings, we hold weekly safety meetings.
 
Behavior Based Safety Observations: We believe that the best way to ensure the success of any safety program is to have our staff participate in behavior based safety observation system. By utilizing this system, it allows us to identify potentially unsafe practices and near misses and focus on training in those areas.
 
Operator Qualifications: We have OQ qualified trained workers, welders, and superintendents. We use a third party company for the majority of our OQ training because it adds to the integrity of the program when performing off-site training.
